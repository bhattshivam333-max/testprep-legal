Privacy Policy

TestPrep Pro

Last updated: May 3, 2026

At TestPrep Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use TestPrep Pro: • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo (if provided via Google Sign-In). • Usage Data: Tests attempted, scores, time spent, and performance metrics. • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification token for delivering course alerts. • Locally Stored Data: Test attempt history is cached on your device using SQLite for offline access.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: • Provide and improve the TestPrep Pro experience. • Track your test performance and show your progress over time. • Send push notifications about new courses and important updates (you can disable these in your device settings). • Authenticate your identity and keep your account secure. • Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

TestPrep Pro uses the following third-party services: • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, and cloud functions. • Google Sign-In — Optional sign-in method. • Expo (by Expo.dev) — App infrastructure and push notification delivery. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: • Access the personal data we hold about you. • Request correction of inaccurate data. • Request deletion of your account and associated data. • Opt out of push notifications at any time via your device settings. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@testpreppro.app.

6. Children's Privacy

TestPrep Pro is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@testpreppro.app
